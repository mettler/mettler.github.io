Gifts

In lieu of gifts, please bring a wish or blessing for Mike and Mollie. It should be a word or short phrase.

During the ceremony, we will pass around the wedding rings and ask one member of each family to share a wish or blessing while holding the rings.

Your wish or blessing should take no longer than 10 seconds to share. If you'd like more time, let us know, and we'd love to have you give a toast!
