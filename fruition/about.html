About

Fruition Advisors advises clients on cash flow and liability management, taxes, risk and insurance management, investments, education, retirement and estate planning. We emphasize balanced, realistic goal-setting and long-term financial health.

Alison Davies, Principal

Alison founded Fruition in June 2013. She was previously an Investor Advisor Representative and Financial Planner at v.h. King Associates. Alison has worked as a Marketing Coordinator at The Wine Group, the second largest U.S. wine company producing 60 million cases annually, and as a Marketing Project Manager at Bonny Doon Vineyard. Alison is a Certified Financial Planner (CFP®) by the Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards and is Series 65 certified by the North American Securities Administrators Association. She holds a Personal Financial Planning Certificate from the University of California Berkeley, an MA in Literature from the University of California Santa Cruz, and a BA in American Studies from Williams.
